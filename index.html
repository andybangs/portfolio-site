Why hello there.

Thank you for taking the time to visit!

I value design as a discipline; it's an exercise of mind, dexterity, and professionalism.

My career first began as an introverted 90’s kid sketching horses & dolphins on my Lisa Frank Trapperkeepers.

Today I'm a visual designer with 4+ years of experience in both agency & freelance settings. My career thus far has involved print, web & physical space——including brand identity, production design, advertising, environmental graphics, image retouching, UI & development, creative writing and all the in-betweens.

Intrigued by the peculiar & mysterious.
Handcrafted, thoughtful results are what you can expect from me.

Currently seeking full-time employment in the Seattle area after living abroad for 2 years in beautiful Kyoto, Japan.
