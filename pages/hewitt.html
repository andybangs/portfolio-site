RALPH HEWITT
REALTOR

brand positioning & identity for Ottawa real estate agent Ralph Hewitt.

The path to finding a home is not the same for everyone, but Ralph Hewitt is eager to create a positive experience for each client. Ralph's congenial & unhurried demeanor creates an instant sense of trust and friendship, which I wanted to capture in his identity. The visual language is sturdy yet playful, honest yet disarming, and perfectly summed up in the tagline: “Let’s find your way home.”
