HIGH MAINTENANCE SKINCARE

photography & website for Seattle skincare studio.

Boasting multiple awards & magazine features as Seattle’s premier skincare studio, High Maintenance was due for a refresh of its decade-year-old brand. I kicked things off with a staff & interior photo shoot, then moved into web design and development, using Robinick’s logomark to inform the visuals. The resulting brand conveys a soothing, luxe, rejuvenating spa experience.
